Elevate Digital Learning with StudentKeeper

StudentKeeper, powered by ContentKeeper, is your all-in-one platform for classroom management, student well-being, and enterprise-grade web filtering. Built to simplify teaching, enhance safety, and empower every role in your school community.

StudentKeeper is built on Ativions award-winning ContentKeeper filtering and real-time reporting platform. This gives school administrators confidence that their hardware and network can manage harmful information that can threaten their students’ data, privacy, and well-being.

Smarter Classrooms, Safer Students

  • AI-Powered Insights: Automatically track academic notes, behaviors, and student concerns to reveal trends and support early intervention.

  • Safe AI Search: Enable students to explore with confidence using our proprietary AI search engine, equipped with built-in safety guardrails.

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Gain instant visibility into student activity across all deviceswhether school-managed or BYOD.

Building a Stronger School Community Together

From teachers and administrators to IT, StudentKeeper supports every role in the school, providing the tools to cultivate a safe, engaging, and productive learning environment. Intuitive, role-specific features ensure everyone stays connected and focused on student success.

Students

StudentKeeper provides a secure space for students to explore and grow.

Lessons and content can be personalized for each student based on their individual needs and interests.

Our proprietary, safe AI search engine incorporates built-in guardrails for students to explore safely.

Teachers

Teachers gain real-time insights to guide and support their classrooms.

Manage student devices individually to enhance engagement, personalized learning, and well-being.

Built-in AI Assistance helps teachers manage academic notes, student concerns, and behaviors.

Administrators

Administrators can ensure compliance and drive improvements.

Inappropriate content can be blocked based on age, community standards, and organizational requirements.

AI reporting dashboards provide data-driven insights to refine personalized learning strategies in the classroom.

Recognized as the Tech & Learning Awards of Excellence: Best of 2024 in Secondary Education, StudentKeeper is trusted by over 1,000 U.S. school districts and protects more than 10 million students worldwide.
